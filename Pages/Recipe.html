Pasta Carbonana:

This creamy dish of pasta in a luxurious sauce of bacon, egg, and chesse is a easy to make treat. Adding lots of good, fresh black pepper will realy add to the brightness of the dish and make the other ingridents "pop."

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound bacon, diced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 pound of pasta, cooled al dente
    • The traditonal noodle is spagetti, but linguine also works
    • Don't overcook your noodles! They will become mushy
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup grated parmeasan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Cook the bacon in a largepan over meduim heat until crisp.
  2. Remove the bacon from the pan and pur off all but 2 tablespoons of the rendered fat
  3. Add the gsrlic and black peppers to the pan and cook until fragant, just a minute or so.
  4. Add the bacon and pasta back to the pan and cook for another minute
  5. Take the pan off the heat and add the eggs and cheese, stir quickly so the eggs thicken but don't scramble.
